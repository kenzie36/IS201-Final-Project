Destinations

The places I've been

Traveling has always been a top priority in my life, it has taught me so much about myself throughout the years. However, traveling is much more than just visiting popular tourist destinations. To me, traveling is so much more! It’s about having genuine experiences that connect me with the heart of a new place. It’s about understanding and interacting with people who call that place home. I am looking forward to the adventures that lie ahead and experiences that will shape me over the coming years that I am dedicating to traveling.

During my travels, I've had the pleasure of exploring:

  • Porto, Portugal
  • Lisbon, Portugal
  • Madrid, Spain
  • Mallorca, Spain
  • Barcelona, Spain
  • Paris, France
  • Annecy, France
  • Milan, Italy
  • Rome, Italy
  • Tuscany, Italy
  • Positano, Italy
  • Cinque Terre, Italy
  • Santorini, Greece
  • Athens, Greece
  • Zurich, Switzerland
  • Vienna, Austria
  • Munich, Germany
  • Cape Town, South Africa
  • Sydney, Australia
  • Cairns, Australia
  • Perth, Australia
To go places and do things that have never been done before – that’s what living is all about.
